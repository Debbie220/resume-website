About Me

I am currently a student at the University of Alberta pursuing a degree in Computing Science with Specialization in Software Practice, and a minor in Business.

I became interested in Computing Science because how influential it was in my native country, Nigeria. Experiencing its positive impacts first-hand, and getting a chance to pursue the passion in Canada, fueled my love for Computing Science. I love discussing IoT innovations and unanticipated consequences of new technologies.

In my spare time, I am a Music Producer and Sound Engineer. I enjoy producing music for the same reason I enjoy building websites and mobile applications - I love bringing ideas to life.
